About the Mission

Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur (SRMD) is a global spiritual movement for inner transformation through wisdom, meditation, and selfless service. Inspired by the teachings of late 19th century self-realized saint and poet- philosopher Shrimad Rajchandraji, SRMD operates through 206 centers across six continents. Pujya Gurudevshri Rakeshji, enlightened master, spiritual visionary and founder of SRMD, blends scripture with experience and intellect with emotion to empower millions worldwide.
